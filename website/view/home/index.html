Introduction

Walk in Bruce Lee's shoes...

BruceLeeMotion is a collaborative art project, as a tribute to our Chinese martial arts master, Bruce Lee.

We provide guidelines for the audience to perform martial arts of Bruce Lee frame by frame; all the performance by the user, is combined together as a stop motion animation, so as to inherit the spirit of Bruce Lee from classic movies.
